Our Facilities

Creating the Right Environment for Learning

Modern Classrooms

Our classrooms are spacious, well-ventilated, and designed to create a comfortable learning environment that supports effective teaching and learning.

Science Laboratory

Faith Academy has well-equipped science laboratories that allow students to conduct experiments and develop practical scientific skills.

ICT Laboratory

Our ICT laboratory is equipped with modern computers and internet facilities to enhance digital literacy and technological skills.

Library

The school library provides a quiet and resource-rich environment stocked with textbooks and reference materials.

Sports Facilities

We encourage physical fitness through organized sports and recreational activities that promote teamwork and discipline.

Safe & Secure Environment

The safety of our students is a priority. Faith Academy maintains a secure campus with proper supervision at all times.

Faith Academy

Faith Academy is a secondary school committed to academic excellence, spirituality, and creativity. We provide a nurturing environment that prepares students for a successful future.
