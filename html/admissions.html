Admission Policy

Faith Academy admits students without discrimination based on race, religion, or background. Admission is based on academic ability, character, and willingness to uphold the values of the school.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Previous school report/result
  • Birth certificate or declaration of age
  • Two recent passport photographs
  • Successful entrance examination and interview

Admission Process

  1. Purchase and complete the admission form
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Write entrance examination
  4. Attend oral interview
  5. Admission offer and enrollment
